Welcome to the Data Science Society at San Francisco State University

We are dedicated to fostering a community of data science enthusiasts, learners, and professionals.

Our Mission

Empower students with the skills and knowledge to navigate the field of data science. Foster a community that thrives on curiosity, innovation, and ethical practices.

Our Vision

Be a leading hub for data science, inspiring a diverse community that collaborates to solve real-world problems using data-driven decision-making.

Join us on this journey, whether you're an experienced data analyst or just beginning to explore the field of data science!
